About Us

We Are ARC International

Welcome to ARC International, the proud home of Heartfulcraft—a brand devoted to bringing the rich, timeless heritage of Indian handmade craftsmanship to the global stage. Founded in 2021, ARC International specializes in sourcing and curating high-quality, eco-friendly products handcrafted by skilled artisans from across India. Our mission is to share the beauty of traditional Indian arts with the world while promoting ethical and sustainable practices in every step of our process.

Export of Premium Indian Handicrafts

At ARC International, we proudly export our premium Indian handicraft products to a wide range of international markets. With a strong presence in the USA, Canada, Africa, UK, Singapore, Malaysia, Hong Kong, and the Middle East , we ensure that the rich heritage and craftsmanship of India are shared with customers worldwide.

Customization and Bespoke Solutions

We understand that every customer has unique requirements. That’s why we offer customization services for many of our products. Whether you need specific colors, designs, sizes, or branding, our team works closely with you to create tailor-made solutions that perfectly match your preferences or business needs.

Customer Support

Our customer support team is always available to assist you.
